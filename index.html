Osman-Foundation Inc.




WHO WE ARE

The OSMAN FOUNDATION INC is a 501(c)(3) non-profit, public charity organization, whose mission is to provide humanitarian assistance to neglected populations.

We are a US-based charity organization whose goal is to provide basic assistance to some of the most vulnerable groups in places like Sudan.

We conduct humanitarian and charity work with a focus on providing healthcare and health services to the underserved, as well as providing education, clean water accessibility and food security to those in need.

WHAT WE DO

We mainly provide financial and technical help for a variety of projects, from funding the construction of schools, to providing access to filtered drinking water.

The Osman Foundation has primarily been self-funded by its founder, Dr. Khidir Osman MD, as well as from the contributions of its board members. However, public donations would give us a greater ability to increase the scope and effectiveness of our activities. If you are interested in contributing to our work, we would greatly appreciate any donations to our organization.

Find out more about our work and the projects we're currently working on through our website, or contact us directly for more information.
